How to Navigate

The pages presented here are intended to be navigated sequentially. After reading and engaging with the content on each topic, you should consider the thought questions that follow and write some notes in order to see how your understanding of grades changes throughout the module. Then, progress to the next section and repeat. Finally, in the reflection section, we will ask you to write a few, short responses to questions to gauge your learning over the course of the module. Thanks!
